College Organizations

BostonHacks

October 31, 2015 - November 1, 2015

BostonHacks is Boston University's top hackathon. For 24 hours on October 31 to November 1, 500 students will come to Boston University, form teams around a problem or idea, and collaboratively code a unique solution from scratch. Imagine and create the most impressive hacks during the weekend! I'm an organizer of BostonHacks.

BUILDS

Sept. 2013 - May 2016

ACM Student Chapter of Boston University. BUILDS is a student-run research lab and workshop that provides BU students with tools and resources for conducting collaborative student-led tech projects. I was vice president of BUILDS (Fall 2014 - Spring 2015).

BUHPC

Jan. 2015 - May 2016

BUHPC is Boston University's High Performance Computing group devoted to discuss, compete, and use equipment for Super Computing events. The group's dedicated for collaboration within BU when it comes to Super Computing especially Super Computing Competitions in the world. We've competed internationally as the Massachusetts Green Team and Boston Green Team. I mentor BUHPC.

Gates Millennium Scholars Program

2012 - 2016

Administered by the United Negro College Fund, the foundation donated US$1.5 billion for scholarships to high achieving minority students. I'm one of their scholars from the cohort year 2012.

Yawkey Scholar

2012 - 2016

A firm believer in equal opportunity, Mrs. Yawkey and the Yawkey Foundations established scholarship funds at Yale University, Boston College, and Boston College High School, was a supporter of the Jackie Robinson Scholarship Program, and supported several other educational institutions to provide minority students and others with scholarship aid. I'm one of their scholars from the cohort year 2012.
